About the Project and the Project's Creator

This website was created in fulfillment of the History Honor's Thesis, Law, Justice, and Society capstone, and Digital Culture and Information capstone at Washington and Lee University. Kaitlyn is a member of the class of 2024 at Washington and Lee University, majoring in European History and minoring in Digital Culture and Information and Law, Justice, and Society. When Kaitlyn graduates from Washington and Lee, she plans to go to law school so she can work as a lawyer pursuing justice system reform in the U.S. Department of Justice. At Washington and Lee, Kaitlyn has been involved in various extracurriculars, including playing on the women's varsity tennis team, being a co-facilitator for FYE-100, and being in the LEAD program. When Kaitlyn is not in class or participating in her extracurricular activities, she enjoys watching sports, playing video games, and reading.
