Georgia's Population from 1860-1970

These graphs showcase Georgia's Black and White population from 1860-1970. These graphs are helpful to compare to Georgia's prison population because it shows that the percentage of Georgia's prison population that was Black was not proportional to the percentage of Georgia's total Black population.
