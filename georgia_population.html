Georgia's Population from 1860-1970

This graph showcases Georgia's Black and White population from 1860-1970. This graph is helpful to compare to Georgia's prison population because it shows that the percentage of Georgia's prison population that was Black was not proportional to the percentage of Georgia's total Black population.
